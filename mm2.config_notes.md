  * X: 210 ( hit motor at left Z )
  * Y: 282 ( could do 286 with a little tweaking )
  * Z: 200 ( could go +20mm if the z motors are switched to the top of the extrusions )

  * mm: 250 x 320 x 3.3
  * inch: 9.84252 x 12.5984 x 0.129921
